Cyber Ale Trail

Explore the world of cyber security with a pint in hand!

This trail takes you to two of the best craft breweries in the city, each with its own unique cyber security twist.

  • Portsmouth - Learn about the basics of cyber security with a flight of beers named after famous hackers.
  • Stockport - Solve a cyber security puzzle to unlock a secret keg.

Portsmouth

Visit Portsmouth Brewery and learn about the basics of cyber security with a flight of beers named after famous hackers. Each beer comes with a description of the hacker it is named after and a fun fact about cyber security.

After your flight of beers, take a tour of the brewery and learn about the brewing process. You can also meet the brewers and ask them questions about cyber security.

Stockport

Visit Stockport Craft Beer and solve a cyber security puzzle to unlock a secret keg. The puzzle is designed to test your knowledge of cyber security concepts. If you solve the puzzle, you will win a free pint of beer.

After you solve the puzzle, you can enjoy a pint of beer and relax in the brewery's beer garden.

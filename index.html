Welcome to the Cyber Ale Trail

The Cyber Ale Trail is a self-guided tour of breweries in the area that have a strong focus on technology.

Each brewery on the trail has a unique cyber-themed experience to offer visitors, from interactive exhibits to delicious cyber-inspired beers.

So come explore the Cyber Ale Trail and experience the intersection of technology and beer!

Breweries on the Trail

  • Byte Brewery

    Byte Brewery is a cyber-punk themed brewery that offers a variety of beers inspired by classic video games.

  • Hackneyed Pub

    The Hackneyed Pub is a cozy pub with a cyber-noir vibe that offers a variety of craft beers and cocktails.

  • Data Point

    Data Point is a brewery that focuses on brewing beers with unique flavors and aromas inspired by data science.

  • Binary Bar

    Binary Bar is a bar that offers a variety of beers and cocktails inspired by binary code.

  • Algorithm Alehouse

    Algorithm Alehouse is a brewery that focuses on brewing beers with complex flavors and aromas inspired by algorithms.
